Design decisions

I am allowing them to enter names outside Neo, Trinity and Joint, just in case they want to keep track of expenses with other people too (but it won't be included in the summation). If they make a mistake, undoing a mistake is very easy anyway (just remove and add again).

I am not giving feedback for wrong inputs (negative number, incorrect currency). Although there could be a solution, since this is to be used by only Neo and Trinity, I assume they know the rules, and the slips are easy to figure out.
